How FIFA impacts us today.

Here you can see one of the most prized possesions in the world, the FIFA world cup. Every four years, the team that has won the world cup gets this trophy. But how does FIFA impact people today? FIFA has brought the world together in a game of soccer and gave many people love and passion for the sport.

What is FIFA?

Better known as FIFA, the “Federation Internationale de Football Association” is an official member body which governs the state of international football, including the biggest event on planet Earth, the FIFA World Cup.

FIFA was founded on 21 May 1904 in Paris with the aim of governing association football internationally. For the 110 years, it has managed to expand to 208 member countries. During its creation FIFA brought together the soccer governing bodies of Belgium, Denmark, France, Netherlands, Spain, Switzerland and Sweden. Germany joined a while later in the year with England coming on board in April of the following year.

FIFA has the duty to maintain the best standars in soccer. It is tasked with administering the world soccer associations. It is in charge of organizing tournaments, promoting the tournaments and governing field matters relating to soccer. The body is responsible for constant development of soccer. It relies on the statutes to govern the system. It is also in charge of the Laws of the Game which are maintained by IFAB.

The First FIFA World Cup

The very first World Cup tournament was held in July 13, 1930 selected Uruguay as host nation and only four European members participating. Italy held the second World Cup in 1934. Since then FIFA has held the tournament after every four years except for two times during the World War II. The body kept expanding over time with members joining from various regions all across the world.
